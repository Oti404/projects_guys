and instrument calibration in an aerospace or industrial environment.

Knowledge of FAA certification processes or military testing standards is highly valued.
A degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, or Electrical Engineering is preferred.
Minimum 3 years of experience in a lab or equipment engineering role.
